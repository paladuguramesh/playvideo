Status Saver For WhatsApp - Privacy Policy

The app provides the facility to view all the WhatsApp statuses images & videos. Provides options to save them to gallery or even you can share them directly without saving. All the saved images/videos can be seen in downloads tab and you can delete them anytime you want.

How the app works:

Open WhatsApp and see the statuses. Come back to Status Saver For WhatsApp and you will see all the status you viewed on WhatsApp here on the Status tab with Images and Videos in different sections. Select any image or video you want to save to gallery as WhatsApp status disappears after 24hrs. Once the image or video is selected then you will have options to Download, Share. You can access the saved image or video in Downloads tab. You always have access to delete the saved image or video from downloads anytime you want.
